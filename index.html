About Us

In today's rapidly evolving industrial landscape, our mission is to transform the efficiency and productivity of your business by automating repetitive and low-skill tasks. We bring to the table an innovative approach to integration, tailoring automation solutions that not only fit seamlessly into your existing operations but also significantly enhance them.

  • Realistic Simulation
  • Fast Prototype and Demonstration
  • Cost Reduction

Utilizing our distinct method of integrating automation into your operations, we commit to a transformative journey with three fundamental steps to ensure success and efficiency in your processes.

Learn More

Step 1: Simulate your business using digital replicas

We begin by constructing a realistic simulation of your business environment using cutting edge photogrammetry and 3D editing. This crucial step allows us to meticulously analyze your current operations and identify the most impactful areas for automation and iterate with you on the best solution.

 
 

Step 2: Development

At no cost for you, we develop the robot code using the functional simulation in house. This allows us to provide 90% of the robot functionalities without even buying one. At this stage we can iterate different type of solutions for business at very little costs and also define pros and cons of different hardware.

 

Step 3: Installation on site

We come onsite and install the robot on your end taking over all costs. In here, we tune the robot to integrate properly on your side so that it generates value for you. We also provide support (free or under payment) depending on costs.
